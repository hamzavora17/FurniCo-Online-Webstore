Our Vision for 2026

Sustainability Goals 2026

Our commitment to a greener planet and ethically crafted furniture. At FurniCo, we believe that beautiful homes shouldn't come at the expense of our beautiful planet. By 2026, we aim to implement groundbreaking changes to drastically reduce our carbon footprint.

100% Responsibly Sourced

Every piece of wood we use will be FSC-certified, ensuring our forests are protected.

Zero Waste

Transitioning our factories to a closed-loop system where all scraps are upcycled.

Carbon Neutral

Expanding our fleet of electric delivery vehicles to cover 100% of shipping.

The FurniCo Eco-Pledge

We are dedicated to transparency. Here is how we plan to hold ourselves accountable in the coming years:

  • Energy Efficiency: Transitioning to 100% renewable energy sources.
  • Eco Packaging: Phasing out all single-use plastics.
  • Product Longevity: Backed by our lifetime repair programs.
  • Community Outreach: Planting a tree for every furniture piece sold.
