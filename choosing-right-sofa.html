Buying Guide

Choosing the Right Sofa

Your sofa is the anchor of your living room. From selecting the perfect fabric to understanding frame construction, our comprehensive guide will help you make an investment that brings comfort for years to come.

Measure Twice

Always tape out the sofa dimensions on your floor to ensure it fits perfectly without overcrowding the room.

Lifestyle Fabrics

Consider performance fabrics or leather if you have pets and children, or velvet for low-traffic luxury.

Frame Quality

Look for kiln-dried hardwood frames instead of particleboard for a sofa that won't sag or creak over time.

Comfort is Subjective

When evaluating a sofa, think about how you actually use it. Do you prefer sitting upright to read, or lounging back to watch movies? Seat depth and cushion fill make a huge difference.

At FurniCo, we offer a range of seating options, from high-resiliency foam that keeps its tailored shape to down-blend cushions that provide that sinking-in feeling. Remember to also check the suspension system—our sofas feature premium sinuous springs for optimal support.

  • Seat Depth: Choose standard (21"-22") for sitting upright, or deep (23"+) for lounging.
  • Back Height: Higher backs support your neck, lower backs open up the room visually.
  • Cushion Fill: Foam for a crisp look, down for casual, plush comfort.
