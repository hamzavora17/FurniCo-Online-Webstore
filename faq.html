Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, delivery, and policies.

Fast Answers

24/7 Support

Clear Policies

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Custom orders may take up to 3 weeks depending on the specifications.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day easy return policy for all standard items. Products must be in their original condition and packaging.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes, all our furniture comes with a 5-year warranty covering manufacturing defects and structural issues.

Can I customize the fabric?

Absolutely! We offer customization for retailers and bulk orders. Please contact our support team for fabric swatches and options.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been dispatched from our warehouse, you will receive a tracking link via email. You can also track your order directly from your account dashboard.

Can I cancel or change my order?

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 12 hours of placement. Since we process orders quickly, we are unable to make changes once an item has entered the shipping phase.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the continental United States. We hope to expand to international markets in the near future!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and select buy-now-pay-later financing options available at checkout.
