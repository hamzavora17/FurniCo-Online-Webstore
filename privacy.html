Detailed Privacy Terms

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us. This includes your name, billing and shipping address, email address, phone number, and payment information when you make a purchase, create an account, or sign up for our newsletter. We also automatically collect certain device and browsing data using cookies when you visit our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information primarily to fulfill and manage your orders, process payments, and arrange for shipping. Additionally, we use this data to send order confirmations, respond to customer service requests, monitor website performance, and (with your consent) send you promotional marketing communications.

3. Information Sharing & Third Parties

We strictly do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to outside companies. We only share necessary data with trusted third-party service providers (like payment processors and shipping carriers) purely to facilitate the fulfillment of your order.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses "cookies" and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your cart items, and analyze site traffic. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies by modifying your web browser settings, though doing so may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of technical and organizational security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information, including SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption for all transactions. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections, or request that we delete your data entirely. To exercise these rights or to ask any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our data protection team via the Help Center.
