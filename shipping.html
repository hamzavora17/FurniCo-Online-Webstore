Detailed Policies

1. Delivery Areas

We currently ship to all major cities and regions across the continental United States. If you live in a remote area, Hawaii, Alaska, or a US Territory, please contact our support team prior to placing your order to confirm delivery availability and shipping rates. Unfortunately, we do not offer international shipping at this time.

2. Shipping Costs & Methods

We offer free standard ground shipping on all orders over ₹40,000. For orders under ₹40,000, a flat shipping fee of ₹4,000 applies. Standard ground shipping drops your items off at the front door of your house or the lobby of your apartment building.

3. Estimated Delivery Times

Standard delivery typically takes 5-7 business days from the date of dispatch. Custom, out-of-stock, or made-to-order furniture may take 2-4 weeks to be prepared and shipped. You will receive an estimated delivery date at checkout and in your order confirmation email.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped from our warehouse, you will receive a dispatch confirmation email containing your tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. Please allow up to 24 hours for the tracking information to become active.

5. Assembly & White-Glove Delivery Services

White-glove delivery and assembly services are available for select large items for an additional flat fee of ₹11,920. This premium service includes delivery to your room of choice, complete assembly of the furniture, and removal of all packaging materials. This option can be selected during checkout.

6. Missing or Delayed Packages

If your order tracking shows as delivered but you have not received it, please check around your property and with your neighbors. If it is still missing after 48 hours, contact our support team. For delayed packages, please note that weather conditions or carrier delays may occasionally impact delivery times.

7. Modifying Your Shipping Address

If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, please contact our customer support team within 12 hours. Once an order has been processed or dispatched, we are unable to change the delivery address.

8. Pre-Delivery Preparation

To ensure a smooth delivery experience, we ask that you measure your doorways, hallways, staircases, and elevators prior to ordering to confirm the furniture will fit. On delivery day, please clear a pathway from your entrance to the room of choice and ensure pets are secured.

9. Delivery Day Requirements

For all freight and white-glove deliveries, an adult 18 years or older must be present at the shipping address to receive, inspect, and sign for the items. If you miss your scheduled delivery window, a redelivery fee may apply.

10. Split Shipments

If your order contains multiple items, they may be shipped from different warehouse locations or have varying lead times. In such cases, your items may arrive in separate shipments on different days. You will receive unique tracking numbers for each package.

11. Order Holds & Storage

We understand that moving or home renovations can cause unexpected delays. If you need us to hold your order, please contact our support team immediately after purchasing. We offer complimentary warehouse storage for up to 14 days once your items are ready to ship. Additional storage fees may apply after this period.
