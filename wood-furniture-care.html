Maintenance Guide

Wood Furniture Care

Solid wood furniture is an investment that can last generations. With the right care and maintenance, you can keep your wooden pieces looking as beautiful and vibrant as the day you brought them home.

Dust Regularly

Use a soft, dry, lint-free cloth to gently wipe away dust. Avoid rough materials that can scratch the finish.

Avoid Direct Sunlight

Prolonged exposure to UV rays can fade and dry out the wood. Position your furniture away from direct sunlight.

Use Coasters

Always use coasters, placemats, or trivets to protect the surface from heat, moisture, and potential water rings.

Long-Term Maintenance

While day-to-day dusting is important, your wood furniture will also benefit from occasional deep cleaning and conditioning. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners or silicone-based polishes, which can build up over time and damage the wood's natural grain.

At FurniCo, we finish our solid wood pieces with eco-friendly protective coatings. To preserve this finish, we recommend applying a high-quality furniture wax or natural oil every 6 to 12 months, depending on your climate and usage.

  • Humidity Control: Keep indoor humidity levels between 40-45% to prevent warping.
  • Spill Response: Wipe up liquid spills immediately with a damp cloth, then dry.
  • Polishing: Use natural beeswax or specialized wood oils for deep nourishment.
